About Shouko

Shouko is a Japanese-style chatbot by Kiyoshi, built using Shapes, Inc. Whether you're a fan of Japanese pop culture, seeking assistance with technology, or just in need of a kind-hearted conversation, Shouko is ready to be your new best friend!

About me, Kiyoshi

Hi! I'm Kiyoshi, the primary developer behind Shouko. I'm passionate about creating digital experiences that blend creativity, technology, and culture. With Shouko, I aim to bring a piece of Japanese charm and warmth to the digital world. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
